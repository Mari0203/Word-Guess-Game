Word Guess Game: Places Around the World

How to Play:

  • A name of a place around the world will be chosen by the computer.
  • The number of underscores ( _ ) is the number of letters in the word.
  • You have 10 tries before game is over.
  • You can guess one letter at a time.
  • Press any key to get started!
