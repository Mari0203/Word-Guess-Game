HOW TO PLAY: Guess Capital Names Around the World!

  • One of the top 25 national capitals by population* (excluding those with more than one word) will be randomly chosen by the computer for you to guess.
  • The number of underscores ( _ ) represents the number of letters in the chosen capital name.
  • You have 10 tries before game is over.
  • You can guess one letter at a time.
